Golf Academy

Golf, learn from the masters

Designed by José María Olazábal, renowned for his short game prowess, Azure Lagoon Golf Academy is a unique golfing experience. The studio offers one-on-one tuition with PGA professionals aided by the latest swing analysis technology.

Our Trackman simulator allows you to play the world's most famous courses, from Pebble Beach to St Andrews, all while enjoying the stunning backdrop of the Indian Ocean.

Diving & Marine

Our secret world

The untouched Noonu atoll is home to numerous exotic dive sites featuring shark cleaning stations, dramatic swim-throughs and elusive Eagle Rays.

A dedicated marine biology team oversees the development of one of the largest coral restoration programmes in the Maldives, and guests can arrange an immersive tour of the coral garden as well as discover the fascinating hidden-gems located within the house reef. The well-equipped dive shop offers PADI certification from beginner through to dive master levels.
